Our Honey Products

Discover our range of pure, raw honey varieties, each with its own unique flavor profile and characteristics.

Wildflower Honey

Our signature blend collected from diverse wildflower meadows. Light amber color with a delicate, floral taste. Perfect for tea, toast, or baking.

Available sizes: 250g,370g,500g, 1kg jars

Clover Honey

Mild and sweet with a light golden color. This classic honey variety is loved for its gentle flavor and smooth texture.

Available sizes: 250g, 370g, 500g, 1kg jars

Heather Honey

A premium variety with a rich, aromatic flavor and distinctive jelly-like texture. Harvested from the purple heather blooms of our moorland hives.

Available sizes: 250g, 370g, 500g jars

Raw Honeycomb

Experience honey in its most natural form. Our raw honeycomb sections contain honey, beeswax, and all the natural enzymes and nutrients.

Available sizes: Individual comb sections
