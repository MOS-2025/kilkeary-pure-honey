About Kilkeary Pure Honey

Our family-run apiary has been producing pure, natural honey for the past 3 years on the beautiful countryside of Kilkeary.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Micheal O'Se, Kilkeary Pure Honey began as a small hobby that grew into a passion for beekeeping and honey production. Today, we maintain over 50 hives across the rolling hills of north tipperary.

Our Values

We believe in sustainable beekeeping practices, supporting local ecosystems, and providing our customers with the highest quality, unprocessed honey. Every jar represents our commitment to excellence and environmental stewardship.

Meet the Team

Our dedicated team includes master beekeepers, quality control specialists, and passionate advocates for bee conservation. Together, we ensure every drop of honey meets our exacting standards.
